FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHAT’S THE NEAREST AIRPORT(S)?

Tampa International Airport, located in Hillsborough County (31 miles/33 min.—varies by time of day), was voted “Best Large Airport in North America” 2 consecutive years. Airport Code:  TPA

https://tampaairport.com

St. Pete—Clearwater International Airport, located in Pinellas County (41 miles/43 min.—varies by time of day) offers limited flights/destination.  Airport Code:  PIE

https://fly2pie.com/

Lakeland Linder International Airport, located in Polk County (33 miles/42 min.—varies by time of day) offers limited flights/destination.  Airport Code:  LAL

https://www.flylakeland.com/

Orlando International Airport, located in Orange County (89 miles/1 hr. 29 min.—varies by time of day) Airport:  MCO

https://orlandoairports.net/ 

WHERE SHOULD I STAY?
There are several hotels in Wesley Chapel + more in adjacent Tampa Bay cities.  Return here for recommendations.
WHAT’S TO DO IN TAMPA BAY?
Wesley Chapel is close to beaches, shops, restaurants, and theme parks.  Other cities include Dade City, Zephyrhills. Tampa, Clearwater, St. Petersburg, Lakeland+.
HOW’S THE WEATHER?
July weather in Florida averages 90 degrees (rarely exceeding 94) and averages 85 on the low end.  There is an average 11 hours of sunshine daily, and UV levels are extremely high.  We recommend sunscreen and hydration.
