# Teresa Edwards - Official Website

The official website for Teresa Edwards, a legendary 5x Olympic basketball player and inspirational speaker.

## About Teresa Edwards

Teresa Edwards is a basketball icon and one of the most decorated athletes in Olympic history. As a 5x Olympian, she holds the record for most Olympic medals won by a female basketball player. Her inspiring journey from Cairo, Georgia (where 7th Street was renamed Teresa Edwards Street in her honor) to Olympic greatness serves as motivation for athletes and leaders worldwide.

## Website Overview

This WordPress-powered website serves as Teresa's official online presence, featuring:

- **Biography & Journey**: Comprehensive information about Teresa's athletic career and achievements
- **Books**: Information about Teresa's published works including "Dream with Faith Legacy Poetry"
- **Products**: Official merchandise from the 5x Olympian, including the Labeled Athletic collection
- **Basketball Camps**: Information about Teresa's basketball camps for high school teams and coaches in Tampa Bay, Florida
- **Speaking Engagements**: Details about Teresa's inspirational speaking on leadership, winning, and overcoming challenges
- **Gallery**: Visual content showcasing Teresa's career and achievements
